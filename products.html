AI Governance

Minimize risk, data breaches and ethics concerns

Harness the potential of AI for your organization while ensuring data privacy, and minimize/prevent data breaches and leaks.

Product Features

Proprietary AI & Data Governance Solution

Polygraf’s cutting-edge on-prem solution empowers your employees to leverage commercially available AI tools and automation while maintaining strict adherence to data privacy regulations and ethical guidelines.

Zero Trust Architecture

Establishes strong identity verification, validates device compliance prior to granting access, ensures least privilege access to only explicitly authorized resources with continuous trust verification and security inspection.

Seamless Integration

Integrates with your existing systems, security stack and AI tools effortlessly. Polygraf AI Suite is designed for on-prem operations with smooth compatibility, minimizing disruption and maximizing productivity

Real-time Data Privacy & Confidentiality Protection

Automatically detects & protects PII/SPI/PCI/PHI or business-critical data with Automatic Personal Information Detection, Customizable Confidentiality Lists & Seamless Entity Replacement, including at point of scan/copy/print.

Secure Data & Encryption

Your data stays local with our on-prem solution – encrypted/masked, processed, and analyzed without ever touching our servers.

Integration with Large Language Models

Enables direct use of the encoded text for any LLMs & AI tools as inputs, enabling secure interactions while preserving privacy.

Multi-Format Input Compatibility

Supports both text input and scanned/printed documents, providing flexibility for users with different data sources, and incorporation into Scanning and Printing solutions.

Persistent Encoding and Decoding

Encode sensitive information, allowing users to use it as input for language models safely. The application stores encoding schemes for future retrieval and restoration of the original data.

AI Source Detection

Protect Originality with AI automation.

Polygraf goes beyond traditional plagiarism detection to ensure the authenticity and original sources of digital content, helping creators, educators, and businesses maintain the integrity of their work.

Product Features

Forensic linguistic analysis

Traces machine-generated text back to the source material that was fed into LLMs with/without the original author’s consent.

Multi-format Support

Supports OCR Transcription, Text documents and files, with a medium-term roadmap to reach Broad Spectrum Analysis covering all text, audio, and visual formats.

Intuitive Interface with In-Depth Analytics

Easy-to-use dashboards & clear scores. Dive deep into potential matches with direct source links and similarity percentages

API Integration

Effortlessly integrate authenticity and source checks into your current workflow, platforms, and content management systems.

AI Content Identification

Redefine Content Authenticity with AI.

Product Features

Proprietary AI Protocols

Real-time Detection
